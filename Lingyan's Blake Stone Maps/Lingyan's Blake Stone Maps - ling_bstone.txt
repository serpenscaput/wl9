Ling's Blake Stone Levels
78 levels for "Blake Stone"
(c) 2008 by Ling Yan Li

Installation:
- Create backups of your original "maphead.bs6" and maptemp.bs6" files.
- Overwrite existing files with the ones from this addon.

Hint:
Some of these levels require you to find barrier switches in secret/super secret levels to get past them.
