aog2.zip

Assault On Germany 2 (21 levels) by Kyo Kusanagi

.......................

Changed graphics, sounds, title screen and status bar.

The levels are 
Level 1-2: tunnels 
Level 3-4: parking lot basement 
Level 5b: Trans
Level 6-10: Prison
Level 11-15: Castle
Level 16b: Airport
Level 17: Hanger
Level 18b: Plane
Level 21fb: Plant of the gold.

To play - Make a copy of the full Spear Of Destiny game. Change the folder name to 
something like 'AOG2'. Then overwrite the files in the AOG2 folder with the files 
from the aog2.zip.
