Episode 3, floor 9.

Prepare for the last level of Wolf-Extra. Open the door and take the chaingun, then turn right and run past wall blocks and columns while being blasted from all sides. Pick up ammo and deliver some in return, but be on the move! There is a pushwall by the health, behind it is the Gold Key and more health. More powerups are found on the other side. But don't wait too long to make it into the hallway. Only to be confronted by two Fake Hitlers. Deal with them but watch out for the officers. Find ammo in adjacent rooms, health behind pushwalls. The enemies may follow you, continue fighting and grabbing their ammo, enter the first room and finish the rest. At this point you are low on everything. Make it past two more Fake Hitlers and enter the grey room. Hold your fire and reinforce. You thought the first section was hard, get ready for the room of death. Fire a shot and have them all come. Fight like never before. You may make it back to the panel area, but enemies are pouring out from every-place now.
