Thank you for downloading the False Spear SDL, a SDL remake of the original False Spear. I have tried my best to retain the atmosphere of the DOS-version while updating the gameplay to the modern standards, so I hope you will find this game as enjoyable as its predecessor.

If you haven't tried the original game yet, I suggest you will try it after completing this one. I am sure you will find it worth of playing as well.

- WLHack (Toni Salonen)
