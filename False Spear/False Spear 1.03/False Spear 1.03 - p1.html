Enemies and objects:

Throughout the game you will be seeing different weapons, enemies and items. Here is a list of the most common enemies and objects you will meet during your journey.

Weapons:

1. Pistol:
A handy weapon to have around... But you should seek for something stronger.

2. MP-40:
Your first choice of weapon for the most of the time.

3. Shotgun:
Slow but effective for clearing room full of enemies.

Enemies:

1. Guards:
The spine of the Nazi warmachine...
They can be found nearly everywhere. When they're alone they don't give much of a challenge, but when there is lots of them...

2. SS:
They present the very elite of the Nazi army... Armed not only with machineguns but also with twisted mind. You don't negotiate with these guys...

3. Officers:
They're fast from their feet and protected with a bulletproof vest. Their only mission is to keep Himmler alive so you probaply need to kill lots of them to get to the top.

4. Mutants:
Devilous weapons producted by doctor Schabbs.
They're as deadly as they're silent so prepare to run.

Health-items:

1. Canteen: +5 health.
2. Food: +10 health.
3. First aid: +25 health.
4. Portable medikit: +25 health (max. four at a time).
5. Field surgeon kit: +50 health ("Final survivor" only).
6. Syringes:
Blue will give you extra life, yellow grants you invulnerability
and red will make you go berserk.

Other items:

1. Spikes: hurts you while you are standing on them.
2. Mine: does massive damage.
3. Detonator: break walls.
4. Backpack: double the ammo.
5. Uniform: disguise yourself.
6. Floppy-disk: extra savestate ("Final survivor" only).
7. Keys: open locked doors.

Treasure:

1. Gold: +100 points.
2. Gold bars: +250 points.
3. Chalice: +500 points.
4. Treasure chest: +1000 points.
5. Crown: +2500 points.
6. Diamond: +5000 points (rare).
