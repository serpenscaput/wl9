Default controls:

- Arrow keys to move,
- Ctrl to shoot,
- Alt to strafe,
- Shift to run,
- Space to open doors and to use objects,
- 'Z' to use portable medikits,
- 'X' to pick/drop dead guards,
- 'C' to put weapon in holster and
- 'R' to reload weapon.
