Part 6

Open any of the brass doors to confront the Devil Incarante! Make him leave his room then rush in and get the spark launcher and the silver key.

Fight him with the spark launcher. Find health chalices in the blue rooms. Every time you open a silver door in a corner room to get more sparks you will release a spectre.

Once brought down the Devil Incarnate will leave you the gold key. And enough poison to rob you of 60 health points. Make sure you have enough health left to stand it!

Find two crosses behind this secret door and gold door. Take one at a time..
