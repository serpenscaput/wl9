Part 6

Within the maze you now enter you will find ammo, health chalices and two 1-ups (one by the "dead guard trick"), but the maze is also filled with vines, pools of poison, death chalices, zombie SS, and the..

..Mutant Schabbs!

The Mutant Schabbs will leave you the brass key but also a death chalice. Make sure you don't make him fall on top of an existing death chalice!

It is best to find this cross (the one outside the brass door) before fighting him since there is a possiblity, although rather a small one, that you may get to kill him while you yourself are trapped at a dead end. continue
