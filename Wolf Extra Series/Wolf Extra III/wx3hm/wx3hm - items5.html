Wolf-Extra III items

 

          




Keys. Open gold, silver, bronze and brass doors respectively. Appear on the status bar.

 

          




Pistols. Come with 4 shots. The one with the silencer is found in parts 2, 5 and 6.

 

          




Machine gun. Comes with 6 shots.

 

          




Chaingun. Comes with 12 shots.

 

          




Missile launcher. Comes with 2 missiles or 20 fire balls/sparks.

 

          




Ammunition clip. Carries 4 shots.

 

          




Ammunition clip. Carries 8 shots.

 

          




Ammunition box. Carries 25 shots.

 

          




Missile. Found in parts 1 and 2.

 

          




Exploding missile. Found in parts 3 and 4. It explodes when hitting an enemy, object or wall and will kill or hurt additional enemies AND you when near it, depending on the distance. Has effect through walls. (Is also used by certain guards and bosses in parts 3 and 4.)

 

          




Box with fire balls. Carries 10 rounds. Found in part 5.

 

          




Box with sparks. Carries 10 rounds. Found in part 6.

 

          




Tin chalice. Adds 10 to your health. Value: 100. Adds to the treasure count. Will be picked up even when your health quota is full. Should sometimes be left for later use.

 

          




Gold chalice. Adds 25 to your health. Value: 500. Adds to the treasure count. Will be picked up even when your health quota is full. Should sometimes be left for later use.

 

          




Cross. Lets you pick up a purple chalice. Appears on the status bar. Value: 2000. Adds to the treasure count. May not be accumulated, disappears from the status bar when a purple chalice is picked up. Extra ones should be left for later use.

 

          




Crown. Value: 1000. Adds to the treasure count. (Treasure only.)

 

          




Chest. Value: 1500. Adds to the treasure count. (Treasure only.)

 

          




1-up. Gives you an extra life, full health quota and 25 shots. Adds to the treasure count.

 

          




Water. Adds 3 to your health when it is 20% or lower. Takes all ordinary ammunition (not missiles or fire balls/sparks) when your health is higher than 20%.

 

          




Poison. Takes 20 from your health. Kills you when your health is 20% or lower.

 

          




Bomb. Takes 50 from your health. Kills you when your health is 50% or lower. Looks very much like an ammunition clip!

 

          




Syringe. Takes 55 from your health. Kills you when your health is 55% or lower.

 

          




Purple chalice. Instant death unless you hold a cross (have a cross on the status bar). Value: 500. Adds to the treasure count.

 

          




Explodable barrel. When it explodes it will kill or hurt enemies AND you when near it, depending on the distance. Has effect through walls. Takes some shots but only one missile before it blows up. Note: Shooting a barrel does NOT add to the kill count.
