Part 1

Give the first barrel a few shots without blowing it up. Then open the door, alarm the mutants and step back a little. When the first few come rushing out blow up the barrels. This will put an end to most of them.

Drop the mutants in the next room by blowing up the barrels. Enter the elevator and get the health chalice.

When you get up here a water puddle will take your ammo. Fight the SS with your knife and get a machine gun. Then fight the roaming officers. Don't open fire, you have too little ammo. The many health chalices will help you out. Get the silver key.

Enter this room and fight these three guards with your knife too (if you have not already done so). Pick up the ammo.

You may now go down to the basement again, locate this secret door and find the chaingun behind a silver key door. Then go back up to the ground floor. continue
