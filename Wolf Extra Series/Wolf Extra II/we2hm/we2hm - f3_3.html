Floor 3.

When you get to the next set of armours you are on the other side of the pushwall near the chaingun. If you didn't get the chaingun earlier remove this pushwall southward blocking the original path. Mutants will come at you, go through the unlocked door to the south and have them follow you through the locked one. Shoot them in the doorway past the skeletons. If you have shot all these mutants previously you can move further south and alarm some enemies in one of the purple/green rooms and have them perform the trick. Any remaining enemy in the areas that you have been through may possibly also fix it.

If you enter the big room with the three Ubermutants in the southwest corner you definitely need to have a chaingun. You can get into this room past a Dead Guard Trick at the south end. To get out again you need the silver key found in this room. To get this key you need to push a wall block. You will probably have to kill at least one Ubermutant before you can start thinking about performing this action. next page back
