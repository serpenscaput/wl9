Other Tools

There are several other tools and programs you can get from http://winwolf3d.tripod.com.

  • WinWolf3D - A Windows-port of the Wolfenstein 3D game. Currently in development.
  • RecolorBMP - An easy-to-use color-changer for 256 color BMPs.
  • SpriteMaker - A simple sprite make that converts multi-sized BMPs to 64x64 BMPs while performing color matching and shading.
  • IMFCreator - Converts MIDI files to IMF. Also allows you to edit the instruments.
  • IMFConvertor - Converts Duke Nukem 2, Cosmo's Cosmic Adventure, and Major Stryker IMFs to Wolfenstein IMFs.
  • WinTExtract - Extracts Duke Nukem 2, Cosmo's Cosmic Adventure, and Major Stryker data. Based on Todd Replogle Extractor by Frenkel Smeijers, 2001.
  • IMFPlay - A simple IMF player for DOS written in QBasic 4.5. Plays Wolf3D-type and Duke Nukem 2-type IMFs. (2000/XP machines need VDMSound)
