Editing :: Adlib Sounds

Priority - Sounds with higher priorities will interrupt sounds with a lower priority.
Octave - Does not relate to a specific musical octave, but serves as a starting point for the frequency data.

For information on how to setup the sound's instrument, see Editing :: Adlib Instruments.
For information on what each sound does, see Editing :: Wolf3D/SOD Sound Guide.

To design a sound, simply draw it in the sound area. Hold the left mouse button to draw the sound and use the right mouse button to erase areas. Each line segment represents 5/700ths of a second (approximately 7ms).

Click "Cancel" to discard all changes.
Click "OK" to save your changes.
