BJ's Revenge

They captured you... they tortured you... and they almost killed you. But somehow, you managed to escape... and now you're angry, and hungry to get even. So you decide to pay a return visit to the place of your captivity, armed to the teeth, to give your former captors a taste of B.J.’s Revenge. But what if they know you're coming, and they’re 'waiting for you...?
