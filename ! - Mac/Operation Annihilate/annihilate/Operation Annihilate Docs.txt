Operation: Annihilate!

The objective of your next mission, codenamed Operation: Annihilate, is relatively simple: to invade a heavily defended Nazi fortress and kill everything that moves. Loaded with ammunition, you blast your way through the ground floor, leaving nothing but death in your wake. But as you reach each successive level, you realize the enemy has entrenched himself in for an all-out fight, and you wonder if maybe you've bitten off more than you can chew...
