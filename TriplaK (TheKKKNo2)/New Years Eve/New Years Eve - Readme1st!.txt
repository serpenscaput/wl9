Project: Wolfenstein - New Year's Eve
By Cateko Protokol (Officer Michael John)

Co-production with TheKKKNo2

----------Info------------
Mod type: Stand-Alone
Other titles: N/A
Construction time of mod: About 4-5 Months
Project Started: April-May 2014
        Ended: August 6, 2014
Engine: Wolf4SDL
---------------------------

Features:
- 60 brand new level
- New GFX and SFX
- New Ceiling colors
- Spear Of Destiny Ammo Box
- Harmless Pacman ghosts (turned to ordinary objects)
- Max. ammount of ammo is 250
- SS always drops Machinegun
- Some Bosses' routines are re-writen
- New Episode names
- And more.....

Known bugs:
Some of the enemies in certain levels doesn't react when they spot you.

How to install/play:

- Create a folder anywhere on your Hard Disk, with any name ("NewYearsEve" for an 
example)
- Copy all the content of the NewYearsEve.rar file to your newly created folder.

- Start game with Wolf4SDL.exe
