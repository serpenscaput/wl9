Wolfenstein 2013

This is a stand-alone map pack for Wolfenstein 3D, created by TheKKKNo2.
Contains:

- 20 levels
- Spear Of Destiny Musics
- Some new Sprites, wall tiles, SFX
- Some code changes: Menu titles are re-writen
- Ammo Box added
- Maximum ammount of ammo increased from 99 to 150
- Some of the bosses and enemies's routines are re-writen
- Silent knife (That's important in some levels!)
- Music order are re-writen

I hope you will like this little mod i've made! Have fun :)
