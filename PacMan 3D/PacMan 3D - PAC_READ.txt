PAC-MAN 3D
a Wolfenstein 3D Mod for LZWolf by AstroCreep

This is a mod of Wolf3D, turning the game into a 3D
version of Pac-Man, functioning more or less like the original
game.  See how far you can last outrunning ghosts that increase
in difficulty with each level!
