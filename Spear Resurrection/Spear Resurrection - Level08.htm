... Level 8 - Easy Does It!

     Rushing blindly into some of the winding corridors in this lower castle level will prove fatal. You'll probably
     need every health item you can find on this level.

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: Navigate the corridor (and explore the office areas along the way for useful ammo/health items) to get the
        bronze key here. Don't forget to grab the MCS Mk3 and the MCS ammo hidden off the small room nearby.
    3: Be careful not to step on green toxic puddles in this room, and watch out for the enemies patrolling
        outside.
   4: From here to point 5 is a bit like running the gauntlet. You can either strafe sideways down this area and
        pick off the clones one by one, or you can try running as fast as hell and gamble that the clones watching
        from the parallel corridor aren't quick enough to react. Watch out though for the suicide clone waiting
        around the corner near the end! The hidden Artefact of Life off this area may be of great assistance.
    5: Caution still needs to be exercised at the end of the gauntlet with a room guarded by suicide clones
        awaiting.
    6: The blood key!
    7: With the blood key, access this area. It may be wise to stand back and let the enemy come to you,
        although you'll still probably have to go in to clean up the stragglers. And although you may be able to see
        the gold key hidden behind a column in the central room, you won't be able to get to it just yet!
    8: Work your way down to this point, where you'll have to proceed to the Artefact of Displacement at point 9.
    9: Jump into the Artefact of Displacement to take you back north where the gold key is kept.
   10: Eliminate the clones in this area and grab the gold key before taking the Artefact of Displacement back
        to point 9.
   11: With the gold key in your keeping, make your way west to this point. Cowardly guards hide behind
        explosive barrels here taking pot shots at you as you pass - if you want to risk the pain of shooting one of
        the barrels to access their hideout, it may be well worth it, as apart from some handy reserves of first aid
        and ammo, this area is the only way to access the Artefact of Life, MCS Mk4 and Artefact of Invulnerability
        hidden at point 12.
   12: You know you want these hidden goodies!
   13: The Elevator key! Plus some nice treasure also! But you'll have to fight for it...
   14: Once you have the elevator key, backtrack through the level to the exit elevator here. On to level 9!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
