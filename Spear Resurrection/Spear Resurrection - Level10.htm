... Level 10 - Hiroshima

     Hiroshima Hyoto holds the gold key you'll need to exit this level! As you'll soon discover, this level has a
     distinctive Japanese flavour that'll have you Turning Japanese!

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: The suicide clones outside can't harm you in here, but you do have to get out sometime! And the only way
        to do that is to stand back and shoot one of those barrels (and hope there's no green toxic sludge residue
        left behind) and then dispose of those suicidal menaces.
    3: This secret area leads to a very handy Artefact of Invulnerability. Use it now, or save it for Hiroshima (if it
        lasts long enough for you to run to him that is)!
    4: Your first port of call is to this room to get the bronze key (unless you visit the nearby toilet block first where
        some enemies are engaging in some rather questionable behaviour). Shooting the officers in the room
        with the bronze key will alert the other officers in the adjoining rooms; however, these guys won't be able
        to get to you just yet...
    5: As you come through the locked door here using the bronze key, the officers you may have alerted in those
        adjoining rooms will most likely come pouring into the corridor towards you. Some may no doubt remain
        behind in the rooms, so be careful.
   6: This room has the blood key. Just be careful when entering the room as static SS guard the area.
   7: The area beyond the columns here has a distinctive Japanese look! You're ready to enter Hiroshima
        Hyoto's lair! The Artefact of Displacement here will warp you to the other side of the columns, but once
        you're there, there's no coming back until after you've eliminated Hyoto and taken the gold key from him.
    8: Once at this point, you have entered Hyoto's Inner Sanctum. The clones in this area are more likely to be a
        greater threat than Hyoto himself, as it's extremely difficult trying to dodge Hyoto's firepower when you have
        pesky clones running around behind you. The best bet is to pick off those clones you can before coming
        into Hyoto's line of sight. So long as you don't cross the green dotted lines marked in the map, you
        shouldn't alert Hyoto to your presence (nah - he won't hear your guns, he's partially deaf y'know)! Grab the
        goodies in those secret areas in the corners (yes, there's an MCS Mk4 hidden there too you know), and
        pick off those clones you can before letting Hyoto see you!
    9: Well, well, well! What's this secret passage hidden behind a torch? It just happens to be the way to the
        Ultra Secret level! In case you didn't know, the torches in the game no longer block a player's movement if
        you shoot them for long enough. This isn't exactly a "feature", but we decided that we wanted to make the
        Ultra Secret level ultra hidden! Oh, and in case you're thinking you can bypass Hyoto by taking this route,
        think again - the gold key is required to access the elevator to the secret level, and yep, Hyoto has it...
  10: Hiroshima Hyoto's lair. A good strategy perhaps would be to run around the corridors outside of this inner
        room, and pick off the clones in the area whilst Hyoto is trying to catch up with you. Once the clones are
        gone, you can concentrate on Hyoto himself. Remember - although the health items are plentiful, Hyoto
        will pick them up as well if he walks over them, making himself even harder to kill. Keep this in mind and
        try not to lure him into areas full of first aid kits. There's a handy Artefact of Life in the central area also, so
        you have no excuse for being killed!
   11: Once you've eliminated Hyoto and taken his gold key, raid his storages of stolen wealth, and then open
        the door to this Artefact of Displacement. Jump in to take you back to area 7.
   12: Enter this Japanese restaurant (remove your shoes beforehand please) and interrupt the clones dining
        there. Grab the elevator key!
   13: This old area was subject to a cave in some time ago and was subsequently sealed off. But the treasure
        items and the Artefact of Life hidden inside may make it well worth your visit.
   14: On to level 11! Or alternatively, proceed to area 15 and on to the Ultra Secret level!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
