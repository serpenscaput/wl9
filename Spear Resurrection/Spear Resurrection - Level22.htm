... Secret Level 3 - Funny Dunnies!

    Ahh yes, some toilet humour... If you thought Spear Resurrection tried to take matters too seriously, then this
    level (found in a hidden area off level 16) should put that idea to rest! A secret dunny level! Perverted. Weird.
    Warped! Dig those sound effects man! Hmmm, makes me think I can't hold on much longer - aren't you
    fortunate there's no smell editor for Wolfenstein?!!! This level is the second creation from AReyeP's 15 year
    old son Daniel (Soulblighter). Perhaps we could explain this level as another "mystery of the spear"...

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: Visit all the rooms off this hallway, as there are valuable stores of ammo and first aid to be found.
    3: Ahah! A hidden Artefact of Invulnerability! Save this for after...
    4: Make your way to this open area, and eliminate all the clones defecating in the area. There's 5 large first
        aid kits in this area - generous!
    5: The bronze key!
    6: This pushwall leads to a large secret forest area. Explore the area thoroughly, because there's an MCS
        Mk4, plus an Artefact of Invulnerability to be found, among other things. Plus don't forget that there's
        another secret room full of treasure just near the Artefact of Displacement located near the end of the
        forest.
    7: Open the door, shoot the barrels, and if any of those clones are lucky enough to survive the blast, finish
        'em. Then, grab the blood key, being careful not to step in any toxic sludge residue left behind from the
        barrels.
    8: Now may be a good time to use one of those Artefacts of Invulnerability mentioned earlier. The Superclone
        that guards this area possesses the gold key - run in and eliminate him, along with all other clones, and
        the gold key is yours. If you don't have an Artefact of Invulnerability, or you feel the one you're using is about
        to expire, then run for the extra one up near the Superclone's throne area (the area enclosed in a ring of
        dunnies, that is...)! Don't forget the wealth of ammo and first aid hidden in those secret rooms off the
        "throne" room!
    9: Whew! It's easy for the S#!+ to hit the fan in this dunny level, ain't it? Well, if you're prepared to cop some
        more, explore this secret area. There's some treasure, plus an Artefact of Life as a reward.
   10: Nasty, nasty deathtrap. But the elevator key is here, so you have no choice but to enter. If you shoot that
        clone in the back (he's facing away from you as you enter the room), then 3 Superclones will open those
        doors to fry your butt for daring to hurt their little buddy! You could engage the clone with a knife (not good
        as their quick reflexes will mean you'll still be hurt or killed), or you could perhaps lure the clone away from
        this room, then eliminate him, which means the elevator key (and those treasures) are easy to get,
        provided you stay quiet, and sneak out like a coward! But, if you want to do it like BJ would, then now's the
        time for for an Artefact of Invulnerability! Grab the one at point 3 (provided you saved it, that is), and run back
        here. Open fire! Eliminate the clone, then the Superclone that comes out of the room with the extra Artefact
        of Invulnerability. Grab this extra Artefact if you need to, and eliminate the other two Superclones. Two
        Artefacts of Life are the rewards for your trouble. And, don't run out of ammo in the middle of this
        exercise, OK?
   11: Make your way here - the exit elevator is just ahead!
   12: On to level 17!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
