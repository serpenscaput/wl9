... Level 13 - The Great Escape!

     Egad! Captured by the nazis and thrown into a prison cell! And with all your weapons taken away from you!
     Things don't look good. But since when does BJ give up? Time to make a great escape from this prison
     compound!

    1: BJ starts here. Yes, it's a prison cell, and you have only a knife. And, if you're on the hardest skill level, two
        SS are standing outside on guard. Seeing all you have is a knife, you have two options. The first is to open
        your cell door, and run forward ever so slightly so as to alert the SS to your presence. Then, dive back
        inside your cell just around the corner of the doorway, and the SS should come in one at a time, allowing
        you to eliminate one with a knife without the other being able to shoot at you. Try to eliminate both SS with
        your knife if possible so as not to draw the attention of guards patrolling the area. Once you've killed the
        SS, you should have a machine gun, and a few rounds of ammo - use 'em wisely! The second choice is
        to open your cell door, and run like hell to point 2!
    2: This secret area is a must visit, as ammo is scarce once you make your "breakout". A pistol, plus some
        clips of ammo, should give you the bare essentials needed to prepare for the areas to come.
    3: The bronze key needed to break out of the prison complex is well guarded by a number of SS. Let 'em
        come out to you instead of rushing in like a fool!
    4: This prison cell is worth a visit, as an Artefact of Life is hidden here. Just blow up the barrel, and provided
        you can take the damage from a possible residue of toxic sludge, the Artefact is yours.
    5: With the bronze key, and your health looking good, now's the time to get out of the prison complex. There
        are a number of ways to exit, but each way takes you into a winding corridor that surrounds the prison
        complex. In addition to the static guards posted in various places, other guards and SS patrol these
        corridors in a clockwise direction, so be alert, and don't get hurt! There are many little hidden areas around
        the corridors - look for them to grab some handy goodies!
    6: If you should discover this Artefact of Displacement, save it for later. Using it before accessing the storage
        compound at 7 will alert enemies making things a little more difficult...
    7: Head to this area, which is the main entrance to a large storage compound. Be warned though that should
        you fire a shot once you open these doors that SS may try to sneak up on you via those locked doors just
        behind you. Clear the storage area of enemies; there's an Artefact of Death that may be of assistance.
    8: Grab the blood key here!
    9: Oh yes!!! A room full of wonderful things! But how to get it? The pushwall acessing this area is blocked by
        a stupid clone on static guard duties! The solution? Simple! You'll have to go to the Artefact of
        Displacement at 6 to warp in to the other side of this room. Once there, the clone will see you - don't shoot
        him straight away though. Let him come running towards you so that he no longer blocks the pushwall,
        then shoot him! Jump back into the Artefact of Displacement that will take you back to area 6, then make
        your way back to the storage compound. You should now be able to move the pushwall, and the goodies
        are yours! Don't forget the MCS Mk4 hidden inside.
   10: With the blood key, access this room to grab the gold key. There's also a hidden area just off this room
        with lots of treasure (plus a few clones).
   11: Access this door with the gold key - leave the other locked door at 14 til after you have the elevator key.
   12: Grab the elevator key here.
   13: A very handy Artefact of Invulnerability. Use this for the next area...
   14: Shoot the guard in the back, and get ready for 2 Superclones to come running towards you! Hope you
        have that Artefact of Invulnerabilty - if not, maybe that Artefact of Death nearby may be of use...
   15: This secret area leads to the Secret Castle level, which is perhaps one of the hardest levels in the game.
        Avoid if you're not into dying!
   16: On to level 14!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
