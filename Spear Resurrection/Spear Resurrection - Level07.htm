... Level 7 - "X" Centric!

     This level is named after the X shaped room that much of the action in this level centres around. This lower
     part of the castle was used mainly for housing Spanish peasants in prison cells, and for general storage.

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: The Artefact of Death in the doorway means you'll have to break one of the stone columns to access this
        room. But, before you do, alert the SS guarding this room to your presence. They'll come rushing through
        the door to deal with you. Just hide around a corner and let the Artefact of Death take care of them!
    3: Be warned, after shooting the suicide clones in this room, the guards in the adjoining room will sneak
        up behind you if you're not careful. Break the column leading to their room, and grab the goodies in there.
        Don't forget the hidden machine gun.
    4: Make your way through the prison area to this torture room where the blood key is kept.
    5: With the blood key in your possession, head to this area. Be careful once in here - SS patrol many of the
        adjoining areas including this hallway! If you don't want to alert enemies in nearby areas, stealth and
        timing may be your best option here.
    6: This secret pushwall in the toilet block leads to a very handy Artefact of Invulnerability - grab this just
        before running into area 7 to make life a lot easier.
    7: This area can be difficult what with clones firing at you from different sides, and with other enemies likely
        to come running out from nearby corridors. If you haven't got the Artefact of Invulnerability from area 6, then
        the best bet here is to quickly follow either the red or green dotted lines to area 8 to grab the bronze key,
        picking off enemies as they come. There's a secret door off the corridor marked with the red dotted line
        that leads to 4 Artefacts of Life - anyone for 200% health?!!!
    8: The bronze key is well guarded by a mix of enemies. Eliminate them and grab the key, which will allow
        you access through all the locked doors in this area.
    9: After grabbing the bronze key, make your way down to the corridor at the bottom of the map, and then
        work your way towards the Artefact of Displacement found here in a storage room.
   10: After jumping into the Artefact of Displacement from area 9, you'll find yourself stuck in a small metallic
        room. Don't worry - just push the wall in front of you and you're free again (not that the clones in this area
        will approve...)
   11: Grab the gold key here.
   12: Jump into the Artefact of Displacement here - not far to go now!
   13: After warping from area 12, you'll find yourself stuck in another small metallic room! Just push the wall
        in front to escape, and yes, deal with the disapproving clones before you blindly rush in to grab the
        elevator key here...
   14: On to level 8!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
