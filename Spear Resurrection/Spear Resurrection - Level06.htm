... Level 6 - Grand Halls

     The wide open hallways in this part of the castle have been used by the nazis in an attempt to create a
     grand "nazified" entrance to the upper castle levels. It won't look so grand though once you've littered the
     halls with bodies!

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: Stock up on goodies here.
    3: Once you open this door and fire a shot, the guys hiding behind the locked bronze door will be alerted
        and will sneak up behind you. Be prepared!
    4: Unless you sneak through the bronze door while it's open, break one of the stone columns here to
        proceed further.
    5: Once in this hallway, be prepared to do some running and shooting, unless you'd prefer to stand in an
        alcove and wait for them to come to you, that is.
    6: This secret area leads to some very nice things indeed, including your very first MCS Mk4! True firepower!
        But, you may want to save these goodies for the last part of the level.
    7: Grab the bronze key hidden in the toilet block here.
    8: Shoot the guards here, and then stand back and pick off the other guards as they come running out from
        the toilet block to see what all the noise is about.
    9: You can wait until you have the blood key to access this area, or, you can access it beforehand by
        breaking some stone columns. Either way, there's an MCS Mk3, a machine gun, ammo and plenty of
        treasure to be collected from this area.
   10: Here's the blood key. But, this area can be a deathtrap if you're not careful. Use the explosive barrels in
        this room to your advantage to help eliminate the enemy, and then the blood key is yours for the taking.
   11: Use the blood key to access this area. Guards patrol the hall and adjoining rooms here, so beware
        of enemies coming out from the rooms adjoining this hallway. Eliminate all opposition before
        proceeding to 12.
   12: The Artefact of Displacement here will take you to the gold key. But be warned! A large number of SS
        wait just behind those doors nearby - as soon as you warp in to grab the gold key, they'll see you!
   13: Now's the time to be quick! Grab the gold key and use the Artefact of Displacement in this area to warp
        back to area 12 (before you do though, don't forget the hidden Artefact of Life here - you'll probably need it).
        Once you've warped back to area 12, the SS will come running after you - now may be a good time to run
        back and grab that Artefact of Invulnerability in the secret area at area 6, or, if you've already used it,
        prepare for a firefight!
   14: Once you've taken care of all the SS, enter this area and break some columns to grab all the treasure in
        this storage room of ill gotten wealth.
   15: Enter second elevator from the left, and on to level 7!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
