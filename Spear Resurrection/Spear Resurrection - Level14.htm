... Level 14 - Forest Gump
    
    This part of the castle is built on the side of a heavily forested hill. You'll need to explore the forest to find the
    key needed to proceed further.

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: Once you exit the elevator, don't muck around exploring - enemies to the south will see you and will be on
        their way! And as if that isn't enough, there's more trouble looming, as a squad of SS on patrol in the north
        are slowly making their way to this area. Run for the hidden health and ammo to help your cause.
    3: Work your way down to here where the blood key is stored. And don't forget to expect those SS to the north
        when you make your way back!
    4: What's this? A hidden area with locked doors requiring a bronze key! Grab the Artefact of Life, and come
        back to this area later if you wish.
    5: Using the blood key, access this area. Of course, you could just sit and wait for the SS to open these
        doors for you if you don't want to bother getting the blood key...
    6: Jump into this Artefact of Displacement to warp into the nearby forest.
    7: Your forest excursion starts here. There's some nice black diamonds to be found, as well as some
        landmines to be avoided!
    8: The gold key!
    9: Once you have the gold key, make your way through the winding forest pathways to the Artefact of
        Displacement here.
   10: You'll be warped back to this point, except now you'll be able to open those locked doors behind you
        (unless you forgot to grab the gold key, that is. Back to the forest if you did).
   11: Heaps of SS guard the elevator key! No time to waste - break the stone column here, and grab the
        Artefact of Invulnerability. Fry the SS, and the key is yours!
   12: The elevator key. There's a handy Artefact of Life hidden just behind it.
   13: Ahah! A hidden bronze key! If you wish, make your way back to the hidden area at 4, and when you find
        yourself facing a Superclone, just remember there's an Artefact of Invulnerability in the treasure room
        he's guarding.
   14: On to level 15!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
