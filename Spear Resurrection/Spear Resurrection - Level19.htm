.. The Final Battle - The Afterlife

     Taking possession of the Spear of Destiny comes at a cost. Your physical being will now have to challenge
     the spiritual "owners" of the Spear in order to break the evil grip they hold over its powers. You thought Adolf
     Hitler was dead? Think again - his partially resurrected being will try to haunt you to your grave in your trip
     here into the "afterlife".

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: As you make your way here, you'll notice Hitler's tomb no longer has a date of death! And where did those
        busts of fallen nazi "heroes" disappear to? And where there was once a room that opened into Baron von
        Schmitt's lair, there is now a caved in wall and an Artefact of Displacement. Jump in to warp to area 3 - the
        final battle awaits!
    3: Welcome to the Afterlife! The ghosts of fallen nazi heroes haunt this realm. Hans and Trans Grosse, their
        sister Gretel, the evil Dr. Schabbs, General "Fatface" and Barnacle Wilhelm may have failed against BJ in
        life, but in death, they cannot be harmed! Not only this, but the haunting red spectre of the Angel of Death
        awaits to exact revenge upon you! Avoid these ghosts at all costs, for mere contact with them will cause
        considerable harm.
    4: The ghost of Adolf Hitler! In a state of semi resurrection back to the physical world, Hitler's spectre is the
        only entity in this realm that can be harmed by your hail of bullets. Avoid his white blasts of ethereal energy,
        and don't focus so much on Adolf lest one of the other spectres in this level sneak up on you from behind.
        He'll take a lot of damage before you can bring him down, be patient, and you'll eventually succeed. And in
        case you haven't figured it out yet, just remember - the walls marked with skulls conceal hidden stores of
        ammo, whilst the walls marked with crosses conceal hidden stores of first aid. And the best hidden
        storages of ammo and health lie not far from where you first meet Adolf's spectre.

        You're on your own now! Good luck, BJ!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
