... Level 3 - Catacombs

    These passages run underneath the main castle complex. Once a part of the castle itself many hundreds
    of years ago, the area was subject to flooding in the wet season, and was abandoned by its Spanish
    occupants of the era. Stone columns were erected in many areas to add support to the roof areas to avoid
    the risk of cave ins.

    The nazis, not wanting the castle breached from underneath by the likes of you, flooded the catacombs with
    clones. Mazelike in structure, these catacombs will be one of your greatest challenges!

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: Break the column to access this reserve of 1st aid - you'll need it.
    3: This room should be your next stop. This mini munitions depot provides you with plenty of ammo for your
        pistol/machine gun, plus plenty of the rarer type of ammo used in the MCS weapons. Yes, there's a
        machine gun hidden near the start of the level, but your first MCS weapon (an MCS Mk3) is hidden here!
    4: Your first foray into the catacombs should be to get the elevator key located in this small opening behind
        a breakable column. Having got this far, you'll have had a small taste of what to expect in the rest of this
         level! Don't forget to grab the ammo and treasure hidden nearby.
    5: Make your way to this area where you can access an anbandoned mine area that leads to the blood key.
    6: The blood key!
    7: After getting the blood key, backtrack through the abandoned mines and make your way to this room
        where the bronze key is kept. After securing the bronze key, you'll have to head back to where you started
        this level.
    8: Once back at this point, break through the stone column, and make your way through to point 9. Expect
        some heavy resistance along the way from cowardly clones hiding behind columns.
    9: Now you have a choice. Head north and secure the gold key needed to access the elevator room at the
        end of this level, or head south, and access the caved in secret area that leads to the elevator to the first
        secret level! Or, perhaps you'd like to do both in order to collect that Artefact of Life located up near the
        gold key!
   10: Elevator to the first secret level.
   11: Should you decide to give the secret level a miss, head back to here, and break through the stone
          column to make your way through to the elevator room.
   12: On to level 4!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
