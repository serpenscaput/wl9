... Level 16 - The Torture Room

     This level is built around a central torture room where hideous cloning experiments have been carried out.
     But you'll have to explore the outer castle corridors first to find the key needed to access this area.

    1: BJ starts here.
    2: Grab the hidden machine gun just outside the elevator should you need it, then head clockwise around
        the level eliminating opposition as you go. Be warned - a squad of SS patrol these outer corridors in a
        clockwise direction. If you have to backtrack along these halls at some time, you may run into them!
    3: The column here will have to be broken in order to access the locked doors requiring the gold key. But
        you'll need to proceed further along the corridors first as the gold key ain't in this area!
    4: This secret pushwall leads to an Artefact of Invulnerability. You may wish to save this for when you access
        the central hub of this level. There's other little secrets hidden off the corridor areas also; look thoroughly
        enough and you'll find a hidden MCS Mk3 hidden not far to the north of this area.
    5: There's a hidden bronze key here. You don't really need this key at all, but it may save time should you
        decide to use the locked doors nearby saving you from backtracking through the halls (and perhaps
        running into those SS)!
    6: The gold key!
   7: Now make your way back to the locked doors here. Jump into the Artefact of Displacement, and you'll find
        yourself in the inner hub of this level. You may wish to run back and grab that Artefact of Invulnerability
        before you do this.
    8: Make your way to area 10 with caution, and beware of clones!
    9: Should you need to teleport back to the outer corridors, use this Artefact of Displacement.
   10: The breakable column here will enable you to access the inner torture chamber. But once that column is
        broken, the 2 Superclones inside will be able to get out - you may wish to try eliminating them before
        breaking this column - the choice is yours. But there's an easy way - see 11.
   11: Ahh yes! A hidden Artefact of Invulnerability! Grab this, and teach those Superclones not to mess with BJ!
   12: The elevator key!
   13: Before you exit this level the conventional way, you may be interested to know there's a hidden blood key
        here which will allow you to access a rather unusual secret level. Just watch out for that clone guarding it!
   14: This series of secret pushwalls leads to a hidden toilet block of all things! But that's nothing compared to
        where that secret elevator off the toilet block will lead you to - the Secret Dunny Level!
   15: On to the Secret Dunny Level!
   16: On to level 17!
 
 































.    Enemies marked in blue appear in all difficulty levels. Enemies marked in green
       appear only in difficulty levels "I'm a Hero" & "Suicidal Tendencies". Enemies
       marked in white appear only in the difficulty level "Suicidal Tendencies".
       Locked doors appear in the colour of the key required to open them, whilst unlocked
       doors are coloured grey (or green if it's an unlocked elevator door).
