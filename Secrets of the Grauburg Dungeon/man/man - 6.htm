Story of Grauburg
Herman tried several times to get money for exploration of these terrains, but he achieved nothing. He lost his interest in Grauburg until February 1938, when a bunch of SS soldiers took him from his bed right to Hitler's quarters.
He entered the conference room. Führer was sitting at the table. "Sit down, please". - he said - "We have to discuss the Grauburg question".
In March exploration began. Führer was known of his interest in magic and occultism. Herman knew, that he was expecting some unusual discoveries. But for the first few months nothing happened. Then, in July, some archeologist found an entrance to the first level of dungeons, then the second and third level were found. But they were empty. The only things found were a little gold, some statues and human skeletons. In August Führer himself arrived at the site.
