Your story
The prisoners were going in front of you. They knew they were going to die. Calmly, they came to the wall. Their eyes were looking at you with hate. You stopped and pulled out your gun. One loud shot. They were all lying dead on the floor. Blood splattered over the wall. You saw Satan in front of your eyes, laughing, laughing, laughing. You didn't even notice when the three other soldiers turned and walked a few steps, chatting with each other. You gazed blindly at the blood, still faces, holes in the bodies. Then someone shouted - "Hey, are you coming, or not?! We have eight more to do!" You turned round and aimed at him with your gun. - "Yes, I am coming. For you, son of a bitch!"
