Story of Grauburg
In June 1940 the new levels were nearly completed. Herman Blausteiner was looking at the digging workers when he saw some gray stones coming out of the ground. "Stop digging!" - he shouted. Archeologists were called to the deepest level. They discovered a staircase and an untouched level of dungeons.
Herman Blausteiner was walking home from the Grauburg's Inn, when he felt that someone is watching him. He turned, but it was dark and he couldn't see a thing. He began to walk faster, then he started running. A moment later he hit into something big and fell down. A black figure came closer to him and crouched. He felt a cold blade on his neck. "So they found out" - he thought.
Next day, the body of Herman Blausteiner was found. It was massacred and his house was robbed. The official version was that he was killed by drunk soldiers. He was buried in a nearby cemetery and three soldiers were executed.
