Your story
It was even worse. You expected to arrive to a place full of lazy scums, just waitng for the war to end. But in the eyes of the people you found in there, you were such a scum. They were real fanatical Nazis, mad Nazis, one would say. But you didn't understand it.
They didn't like you. You were punished and laughed at. You soon found out, that it was because you were in Grauburg because of your father, who didn't want you to die in this war. He managed to settle your transfer to Grauburg with a friendly officer.
Willing to repair our image, you began to be more and more cruel to the prisoners and started to find a pleasure in doing that. You were doing all your duties in the best way you could. Soon, other soldiers began to change their opinion about you. You even found some friends. And then it begun...
