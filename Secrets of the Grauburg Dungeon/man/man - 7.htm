Story of Grauburg
After examining the dungeons, he returned to his quarters. There was a need for a secret intelligence base and officers residence. Hitler decided for Grauburg, but he still felt there's some mystery to be discovered in those dungeons. Soon exploration was stopped and a builder crew arrived at the site. The ancient dungeons were converted into a intelligence base, with a small prison for enemies important because of their knowledge.
In November 1939 a guard was walking on the deepest level of the base when the floor collapsed under his feet. He fell into a small room with a staircase entrance. The fourth level was discovered! Scientists soon found entrances to two deeper levels, which were much more interesting than the first three.
But there were more and more prisoners coming to the base and there was not enough space for them, so at the beginning of 1940 Hitler decided to build two additional prison levels.
