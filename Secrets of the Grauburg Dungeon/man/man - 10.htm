Your story
"How stupid I was!" - you are thinking now. In September 1939 the war began. Thousands of German soldiers attacked Poland. It seemed so beautiful to fight, fight for a better life for your children. But it didn't last for long. You were hurt in your leg and chest during a small battle near some little village. You were taken to a field hospital. There you saw human pain and suffering. But you still could understand it, you were proud of being hurt for your country.
It took you five months to get back to full health. How disappointed you were when a message came for you. You were being moved to some village in the Alps, where you would serve as a guard. So there wouldn't be battles anymore. No more fighting! You would sit in some chair, listen to the radio and get bored. There probably wouldn't even be any pretty girls. Grauburg, a hole of a place!
