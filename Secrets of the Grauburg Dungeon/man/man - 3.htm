  2. Totally new story
  3. Manual (you've probably noticed)
  4. Nearly all the in-game graphics have been replaced
  5. New menu graphics
  6. New title screen and status bar
  7. Many new sounds
  8. New music order
  9. New game feeling (at least I think so)
