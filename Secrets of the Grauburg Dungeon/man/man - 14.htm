Your story
A week after the archeologist's death some new scientist arrived. Soldiers told you he was one of Himmler's best friends, a well known SS officer and historian. After his arrival, only his trusted guards were allowed to go to the deepest level. A lot of scientists came with the officer. They were asking about anything unusual. You wanted to tell them about your dreams, but the following day, every guard, who saw the violet light, wasn't in his bed. Strangely, you knew they were dead.
Keeping your symptoms a secret had been a good idea. Someone, who was on his pass in the town, said he saw the bodies of the missing guards being packed on a truck and taken somewhere in the mountains. He probably was lying, but it really didn't matter. Your visions were too strong. You could not think. The only thing you wanted to do was to escape from this hell. Where to? Mountains, forest, allies prison or even grave. Anywhere, but not here!
