Menu/Intermission Screen Sounds
SHOOTSND When the player selects an item in a menu.
 
MOVEGUN1SND
MOVEGUN2SND		 When the gun is moved in the menus.
 
SHOOTDOORSND When the player changes a control key in the control menu.
 
ESCPRESSEDSND When ESC is pressed in a menu.
 
ENDBONUS1SND Played in the intermission screen as the ratios are counted.
 
ENDBONUS2SND Played in the intermission screen when a ratio is between 1% and 99%.
 
NOBONUSSND Played in the intermission screen when a ratio is 0%.
 
PERCENT100SND Played in the intermission screen when a ratio is 100%.
 
Bonus Item Sounds

GETGATLINGSND When the player picks up a gatling gun.

HEALTH1SND When the player picks up dog food or regular food.
 
HEALTH2SND When the player picks up a med kit.
 
SLURPIESND During Hitler death animation, or when you drink blood if you are under 11% health.

BONUS1UPSND When the player picks up an extra life.
 
GETSPEARSND When the player picks up the Spear of Destiny.
