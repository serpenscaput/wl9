Map plane manager

Select plane

If you want to switch to a different plane, simply click on the drop-down list on the left side in the plane manager, and select the plane you want to edit.

Add / delete plane

If you want to add or delete planes change the value in the bottom left section, click on the "Change number of planes" button, and after that click on "OK".

Plane name

Here you can change the plane name.

Change plane properties

If you want to make a plane visible or invisible, simply click on "Show plane", and after that click on "OK". Now you have made the specific plane visible or invisible.

If you want to activate or deactivate "Low and High byte"-support for a plane simply click on "Split into low and high bytes", and after that click on "OK". Now you have activated or deactivated "Low and High byte"-support for the specific plane.
