PAC-MAN: Extendo Maze
11/25/2020 AstroCreep

This is an add-on for Pac-Man 3D for LZWolf, swapping
the original maze out for a much bigger one, with 
8 ghosts instead of 4.

Also includes the soundtrack from PacMan CE.

To run, simply load this pk3 alongside the Pac Man 3D pk3

example:

c:\path\to\lzwolf.pk3 --file PacMan3D.pk3 --file Pac_Extendo.pk3

OR

Open a command prompt box, drag your LZWolf exe inside,
then drag PacMan3D.pk3, and finally drag Pac_Extendo.pk3
and run.

MAC INSTRUCTIONS:

Open a terminal window, drag the LZWolf app inside,
type \Contents\MacOS\lzwolf onto the path, drag
PacMan3D.pk3 and then Pac_Extendo.pk3

example:

/Applications/LZWolf.app/Contents/MacOS/lzwolf /Users/astrocreep/Documents/ECWolf/Mods/PacMan3D/PacMan3D.pk3 /Users/astrocreep/Documents/ECWolf/Mods/PacMan3D/Pac_Extendo.pk3
