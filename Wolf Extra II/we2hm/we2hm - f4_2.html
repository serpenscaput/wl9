Floor 4.

Now you enter the central section. Eliminate the two mutants and pick up some more goods if you need it (this may be fetched later). Beyond another Dead Guard Trick you find even more. Near by is a secret door that you may get through from the other side. Enter the door to the central block, a last Dead Guard Trick blocks the retreat. Move through the next door and get ready for a countdown with the Death Knight and a whole lot of officers and SS. Keep moving, pick up health and ammo while finishing with the officers and SS, and fighting off the Death Knight. Locate the secret doors. A silver key is in the central niche, this lets you out into the northern passage. Defeat the Death Knight and get the gold key. Two more silver key doors leads to the opening section. If you get stuck in between the silver key doors with the Death Knight at your heels and move up here watch out for the guards behind the columns, the purple brick areas will now be blocked off. The Death Knight at last defeated try to finish with these guards as well. Then you enter the gold key door behind the entry elevator for the Spear. back
