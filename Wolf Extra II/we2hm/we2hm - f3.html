Floor 3.

There is a chaingun hidden in this level and there are two ways of getting at it.

To get it the fast way you must do as follows. Open the first door, then turn to the left. You will see a mutant entering a locked door. Shoot him in the doorway but beware the mutants behind the barrels to the north. Run through the now opened door, turn right and go through the next door. You will then see a second mutant entering another locked door. Don't shoot him but move up to the columns in time to see a third mutant opening yet another locked door. Shoot him in this doorway and wait for the second mutant to come at you. Shoot him to get past the second locked door and move over to the third one.

There's a pushwall behind it. Remove this and follow the path to the left and the chaingun is yours. next page
