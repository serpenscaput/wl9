Floor 4.

Pick up the ammo and the machine gun by the locked door. If you start this level with only a little ammo (i.e. 8 shots) don't waste anything on the enemies behind the columns (you may handle them later). For a chaingun move into the northeastern section and get involved in some heavy action to acquire one. Then run back past the columns and enter the northwestern section. A Dead Guard Trick blocks the passage back. Fight the enemies in this section then enter the room with the prison cell. Push this secret door. Now you enter the big blue stone area with a lot of similar looking corridors. Start exploring this area. Be prepared for surprises. But be very careful not to waste too much ammo, there is not much to be picked up. Try to locate the extra ammo that exist. Over in the eastern part there are two movable wall blocks both situated by green barrels. The southern one lets you get into a small part of the central area, here you find a lot of ammo. The other one is a Dead Guard Trick, beyond it you enter further into the central parts. But locate first the prison cell in the northeast, a 1-up is found inside it. next page
