Castle 5

Make it to the silver key.

Continue northward through the eastern section. Find the brass key here.

Find and fight your way westward through the northern section. Find a secret room here.

Kill as many as possible by blowing up the barrels. Finish with the rest, and move on through the door.

Back in the big room, but where is the General? Push the wall block for the bronze key, and the General himself. The missiles that flew your way previously came from (now dead) super officers that noticed you at the same time as the General.

The bronze key will let you into rooms with powerups. But more enemies are now coming. Be on the move and fight for your life. Avoid the death chalices! There are no crosses on this floor.

Finally defeated the General will leave you the gold key for the elevator. Shooting the barrels will put an end to the guard. back
