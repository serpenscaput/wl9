The Tower enemies

 

          


"Sub" Mutant. Carries no gun, attacks with cleavers. Takes more damage than a guard, but not as much as an officer. Kill value: 200. Leaves nothing. Special note: Will not open doors.

 

          


Guard. Attacks with a pistol. Kill value: 100. Leaves a pistol.

 

          


SS. Attacks with a machine gun. Takes twice as much damage as an officer. Kill value: 700. Leaves a machine gun.

 

          


Officer. Fast moving. Attacks with a pistol. Takes twice as much damage as a guard. Kill value: 400. Leaves a pistol.

 

          


Mutant. Fast firing. Attacks with pistol and cleavers, very dangerous on close quarters! Takes more damage than an officer, but not as much as an SS. Kill value: 600. Leaves an ammunition clip.

 

          


Super SS. Attacks with a chaingun. Takes one and a half times as much damage as an ordinary SS. Kill value: 1000. Leaves a chaingun.

 

          


Super officer. Very fast moving. Attacks with missiles. Takes four times as much damage as an ordinary officer (twice as much as an ordinary SS). Takes two missiles before he goes on "Death Incarnate". Kill value: 1300. Leaves a missile launcher.

 

          


The General. Attacks with missiles and a chaingun. Takes twice as much damage as standard General Fettgesicht. Appears once. Kill value: 5000. Leaves a gold key.

 

          


The Giant. Very fast moving. Attacks with sparks and a chaingun. Takes more damage than the General (two and a half times as much as standard Grosse). Appears twice. Kill value: 5000. Leaves a gold key or an end level object (on secret castle).

 

          


Supreme Soldier (Death Knight). Attacks with two missile launchers and chainguns. Takes more damage than the Giant (more than twice as much as standard Death Knight). Appears once. Kill value: 5000. Leaves a gold key.
