Floor 8.

Turn around and get the Missile Launcher. Some guards will notice you. Forget about fighting your way to the south through that pack of enemies which includes an Ubermutant. Pick up as many missiles as you can and go through the entrance elevator door. And confront a second General Fettgesicht! Be on the move. And make sure to wade through all the missiles that are scattered on this floor. There is no ordinary ammo to be found (except what is dropped by the enemies). Again enemies will move in from every direction. Fake Hitlers are found both in the north and the south. Get the brass key in the central northern room. Move on to the southeast outdoor area for the gold key and a third Fake Hitler! The northwest outdoor area holds the bronze key, the northeast one holds the silver key behind a pushwall. 1-ups are found in three other secret rooms. After battling the other guards you move in for a missile launching duel with the General himself. The elevator is found in the southwest outdoor area. Watch out for the mutants on the outside of the elevator (as in the other outside areas).
