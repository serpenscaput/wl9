Floor 6.

To get a Missile Launcher shoot the mutant in the doorway, run through the next door and shoot another in the next. If you don't you will still get it once you have the gold key. Start by a dead guard trick and enter the central green moss section. Soon an Ubermutant will discover you. Get the bronze key in a little room. Several passages are blocked by poison. On this floor as well as others, enemies may come unexpectedly at you through these passages. Pick up enough health chalices to get on southward for the gold key. In the northwest you find the silver key, a chaingun and another Ubermutant! If you enter the northern section without a gold key you must make it back through poison, find chalices in a secret room. From the northern section you enter the southeast area. Or find a secret shortcut (with a 1-up) from the central area! Here you confront Dr Schabbs. More enemies will come in from other areas. Finally defeated Dr Schabbs will leave a nasty surprise! Get the brass key and find the elevator behind a pushwall, again there is a pool of poison inside it. Two more 1-ups are hidden on the floor.
