SGE - 10 shareware levels, plus separate saved game:

This 10 level set for shareware Wolf 3D was made by N. Korroveen. Also included is 
a saved game called 'Prison' that is not on any of the levels. This set, along with 
another saved game that can be used with the registered Wolf 3D set, is also included 
in his S_G_E Wolf3D Editor package. The complete package is available at http://www.brlowe.co.uk/sge.zip.
