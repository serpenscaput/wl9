Wolf Hour + Epilogue 4SDL


Thank you for wanting to play Wolf Hour + Epilogue.

In order to play Wolf Hour + Epilogue you need to own a registered version of Spear of Destiny by ID Software. Copy the AUDIOHED and AUDIOT files from Spear of Destiny to Wolf Hour + Epilogue and change the extensions accordingly:

audiohed.whr
audiot.whr


This edition of Wolf Hour includes the previously released 1 level game Wolf Hour Epilogue.
Wolf hour + Epilogue has a total of 11 levels including 2 secret levels.
The "Death Incarnate" difficulty level is designed to be as difficult as possible without being impossible!
It is possible to get the 100% ratio in all three categories on all levels, including the boss- and secret levels.
All levels have been tested and won on "Death Incarnate" with total ratios of 100% in all three categories.

Remember to leave saved games in different slots so that you may go back to different stages of the level you are on if you need to.
