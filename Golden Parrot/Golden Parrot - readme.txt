Golden Parrot is a pirate-themed Wolfenstein 3D mod, embark now!
9 weapons, improved controls, merchant system, and more...
Choose between a english, french, spanish or dutch privateer and get ready for adventure!

Yaaarrrr!


CONTROLS
--------

W, A, S, D, Mouse, Arrows: Move player
ESC: Menu
ALT: Strafe
CTRL: Primary attack
Spacebar: Open doors, interact
SHIFT: Run
Mouse left button: Primary attack
Mouse right button: Secondary attack
R: Reload weapon
TAB: Display Map (requires map item)
J: Display weapon expertises
K: Display skill statuses
L: Display ratios (requires logbook skill)
1-9: Weapon selection
E: Display skills catalog


GAMEPLAY INFO
-------------

*Nation bonus

English: Magic oriented: gains +1 mana per potion, 
                               +5 mana per holy chalice,
                               +15 to maximum mana

Spanish: Defense oriented: -1 damage taken from every source

French: Fortune oriented: improved loot rate in crates, 
                          improved ammo loot on imperial soldiers, 
                          passive dodge 5%

Dutch: Attack oriented: +2 damage to close attack
