Dungeons 3

Put an end to the guard with your knife and get his pistol. Enter the room to the south and shoot the two mutants. When you pick up the upper missile an SS will notice you from behind the wall.

Go here and shoot him in the silver doorway. Kill the other guards and get the gold key. But don't shoot the deaf mutant you see to the south!

Rush through the room to the north, don't shoot the SS! When in the upper hallway shoot when you hear him open the door behind him.

An officer will then come through this silver door. Shoot him in the doorway. Kill the SS by shooting the barrel. Follow this path to a cross.

Stop by the crown and shoot the barrel by the bronze door, this will wake up the SS on the other side. Get back in time to shoot him in the brass doorway. You may now enter the elevator, look out for the guard.

To explore the rest of the floor:

From the north, shoot at but don't kill the deaf mutant. Then run south again and kill him in the bronze doorway. Get the brass key beyond it.
