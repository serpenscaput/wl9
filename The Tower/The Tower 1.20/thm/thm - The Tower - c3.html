Castle 3

Get the chaingun and the missile launcher near the start. Run over here for some ammo and shoot the enemies behind the columns to the right.

Find more reinforcements over here. Know that a super SS may come unexpectedly at you from the bronze door to the south at any time.

Take up position near the gold door. Shoot the leftmost barrel you see. This will wake up an SS. Shoot him when he opens the brass door..

..and run back here shooting the guards that suddenly appear through the silver door. See to it that this silver door remains open. If the brass door remained open you may clean out the left central room.

Go beyond the silver door and fight your way northward through the eastern area. Pick up the brass key here on the way. Watch the mutant!

Fight for the silver key in this room.

Clean out this room, but look out for the super officers coming from the red brick room in the south! Find the gold key behind the ventilator.

Go southward through the western area. Fight to get to the bronze key.

Fight for your life in this area to get the cross in the north western room. It will let you into the blue brick room. There is a death chalice in the elevator. Find crosses in the nearby secret area, but watch that mutant!
