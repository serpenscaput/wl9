Castle 2

Find the brass key in this room.

Open this secret door. Fight to get to the cross and the health chalice.

Find the bronze key in this room.

Make it past this pushable block on your way to the elevator. Shoot the barrels in the next room and kill the super officer. Look out for all the super SS coming from all directions. Remember to have the super SS in the elevator open the silver door.

The secret area:

Have enough health to get past the poison. Beyond the two pushwalls you lose your ammo. The five missile launchers make sure you will have exactly 10 missiles. You must kill all three super officers in the long corridor by blowing up the barrel in front of each of them, otherwise you will not have left enough missiles for all the explodable barrels ahead. And there is no way back!

Beyond another "Dead Guard trick" you eventually get to this room. Alarm the guard but don't open fire. Get past the secret door, pick up ammo and missiles and make it through the next secret door.
