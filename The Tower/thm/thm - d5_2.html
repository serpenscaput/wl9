Holding a cross, go north by east. Get past a "Dead Guard Trick" and a secret door and get the brass key in this cell. Then continue eastward past a death chalice.

When the action starts in the south eastern hallway a super officer and two super SS will come at you from behind. Avoid stepping on the poison, and the water unless your health gets down to 20% or lower.

Open this door, shoot the officer and the barrel ahead, then the mutant coming through the door ahead. Rush past the SS and super SS, one SS will follow you. Follow this path back to the western area.

Shoot the enemies and barrels in this room and take the health chalices. You need to get through the poison filled western cells for the gold key.

Shoot the barrels and finish off the enemies in the central hallway. Find more health in one of the eastern cells, but watch out for the mutant by the death chalices.

Collect enough health to make it to the gold key in this cell.

Shoot the barrel by the gold door, open it and push the wall block. A super officer will immediately come at you from the elevator. This super officer may open the elevator door before you push the wall, in which case it will be possible to push the block eastward (and blocking the elevator).
