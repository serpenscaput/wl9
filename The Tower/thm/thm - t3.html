Tunnels 3

Get the pistol and ammo to the south, wait for the SS and shoot him.

Run west by north. Kill the super SS, then fight the super officer to acquire a missile launcher. Get the extra missiles quickly, a second super officer is coming from the south. Watch out for other enemies as well.

Pick up this health chalice and shoot the mutant past the poison. Then push the wall for the silver key.

Make it to the big room in the north west, kill the guards by blowing up the northernmost barrels. Don't shoot the barrels by the bronze door yet!

Push the first wall for the brass key then the second one, fight the guards in the next room. Now someone will have come out of the bronze door.

Shoot the barrels while the bronze door is open to get to the bronze key.

Find the gold key here. (You can get to it from two different angles.)

Two death chalices block the path to the gold door past this pushwall. Take with you one cross at the time!

Shoot the barrel that blocks the path, rush forward and to the north, pick up the health chalice. Turn around and blow up the remaining barrels to kill the SS, two super SS follow. Don't get surprised by the super officer in the elevator to the secret tunnel.
