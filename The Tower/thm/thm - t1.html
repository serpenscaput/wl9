Tunnels 1

You start here.

Take out all the "sub" mutants with your knife. Stay unnoticed by the mutants hiding in many places. Locate the health chalices.

Find a cross here, but look out for the SS to the north!

Pick up the bronze key here, behind a death chalice and a pushwall.

Collect what ammo you find, but look out for fake ammo clips. Open a door to the guards' room, kill one with you knife, get his gun and shoot the others. Now you may finish off the mutants as well.

Drop this mutant and get the silver key.

Find the gold key behind this pushwall. You must wade through some poison, there is a health chalice behind it. Shoot the SS.

Blow up this barrel from the east to avoid getting hurt.

Fight the officers in the last room, shoot the barrels. Then make it for the elevator.
