Castle 4

All pushwalls in the main part of this level have skulls. (And all skull walls are pushwalls.)

Fight the first few enemies you encounter, then enter the maze and finish off with any guard you find there (two mutants, an SS and a super SS).

Locate this pushwall..

..and go north and west past several pushwalls, killing a "sub" mutant on the way, to find the bronze key here.

Shoot the barrel from the north past the column. Go through the bronze door, push the wall. Push the wall by the exploded barrels eastward, get the cross. Take the rightmost death chalice and push the nearby walls.

Go south and west till you get to this room. By the crown you alarm a mutant. Shoot him in the brass doorway and get the brass key beyond it. Make it back through the brass doors in the north.
