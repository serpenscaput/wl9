Dungeons 1

You start by losing your ammo.

Find a pistol here. Important! Don't take the nearby ammunition box! Entering the central room from the south will prove impossible.

Shoot this "sub" mutant, and the guard when he opens the silver door. Take the cross, but again leave the ammunition.

Get the silver key here and go through the nearby silver door. Your ammo will go, kill the guard past the "Dead Guard Trick" with your knife.

Get the bronze key, and a super SS chaingun in this room.

Make it back through this passage. And lose your ammo again.

Tour the floor counterclockwise and get the brass key here.

This chalice has ensured that you have not drunk that water earlier. Lower your health in the poison room to drink all this water.

Find a missile launcher and missiles past this pushwall.

Open this door and start the action by shooting the barrels ahead.

Later find a cross here to make it to the gold key up north. The health in the southern room lets you pass the bombs on your way to the elevator.
