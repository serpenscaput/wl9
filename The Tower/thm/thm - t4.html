Then past the next one to the east.

Make it here for the bronze key. Then push the wall by the health chalice. Kill the mutant behind it. Open the door to the north.

Kill the guards coming out of the door ahead. Shoot the barrels in that room to kill the super SS.

Go here and shoot the barrel by the death chalices to wake up the mutant. Shoot him and push the wall.

Blowing up the barrels past the next door will be difficult if you have not already done so (see the first paragraph). Don't shoot the mutant ahead but have him open the silver door, then shoot him in the doorway. Get the brass key.
