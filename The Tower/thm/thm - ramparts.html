Ramparts

Get the guns and fight the enemies in the blue hallways.

Enter the ramparts through one of the northern doors.

Tour the ramparts which way you want. Both ways are rather tough.

Encounter the Giant here. The brass key is past the door behind him.

Find the bronze key here, past a pushwall and a brass door.

Find the silver key here, past a bronze doors.

Find reinforcements in many rooms and secret rooms. Look out for bombs (fake ammo clips), but..

..gain 75 then lose 50 of your health by stepping on this bomb. Take the cross (and the crown).

Defeat the Giant ultimately, and get the gold key.

Open the secret door situated a little to the south. Shoot the two barrels. Open one of the silver doors, run past the secret door to the south, open the other silver door and push the wall block behind it while the first silver door is still open. Go past the death chalice. Open the gold door. Take the Rune Stone.
