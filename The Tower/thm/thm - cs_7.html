Secret castle

Open either of these gold doors, but preferably the upper. Fight the super SS. There are health chalices in the room. A super officer will come from the central room. Defeat him also.

Then take up position here and open the door. For a first encounter with the Giant.

Fight him while running around the central room. When necessary go down to the other floors for reinforcements (in the rooms with gold doors). There are some extra ammo in the central room on the roof.

Finally defeated the Giant will leave you a teleporter taking you to floor 15 (castle floor 5).
