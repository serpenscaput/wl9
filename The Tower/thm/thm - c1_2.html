Castle 1

Find your way southward and past a "Dead Guard Trick". Then start fighting your way towards the west through the southern part of the floor.

Continue past another pushable block.

Past a gold swastika pushwall you find this room with two crosses. After defeating the enemies you may take one cross for the death chalice in the room a little way back. Then come back for the other cross.

Go north past this set of pushwalls.

Make it here, watch the enemies behind the columns. Shoot the barrel and enter the central room. Battle the three super SS. More enemies will be alarmed in other rooms.

Push these wall blocks, shoot the barrel and get the gold key.

Then push this wall. Open the gold door, shoot the barrel and kill the super SS if you have not already done so. The additional crosses will let you collect the remaining death chalices on the floor. But look out for unexpected enemies coming at you. back
