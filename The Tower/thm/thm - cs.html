Secret castle

You start here. Take the two guns. Fight the enemies in this first area. Pick up all the missiles and ammo.

You may explore the many floors, rooms and surrounding areas of this castle in many ways. There are enemies to fight in all areas. Some may come at you quite unexpectedly. Enemies in some rooms may be alarmed at varying moments. The following description gives you the fastest way to get through. In most cases backing into elevators is the safest way. NOT so with one way cell doors.

(In the description 1st floor is the floor above ground floor, 2nd floor is the floor above 1st floor etc.)

Go down to the dungeons here.

Find yourself here. (Scroll down a little.)

Take the lower of the one way cell doors and the lower of the elevators.

Kill the mutant and go down again.

Move around and take the upper of the elevators.

Get the cross.

Go down again and make it here for the elevator to the ground floor.
