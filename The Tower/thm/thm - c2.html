Castle 2

The door is locked. You have to wait for the moving guard to come and open it. Defeat him but watch the SS standing to the left, right outside.

Find a chaingun and a missile launcher here. Leave the barrels outside.

You must get past all four locked doors in the central area to get to the elevator. Note that there is no silver key on the floor, you need to have the super SS in the elevator open the silver door. The keys are found in rooms that are blocked by death chalices. There are three crosses you may find in any order. Likewise the keys may be picked up in any order. Notice that there are additional rooms blocked with death chalices. It is vital that you don't pick up any wrong death chalice. The following describes one order in which you may get the crosses and the keys.

Make sure you are low on ammo, or with 20% or lower on health, when passing the water. By the upper left missile you alarm the SS. Shoot him in the silver doorway and get the cross. Two pushwalls let you find the way to the health chalice behind the vase. One more lets you back.

Find the gold key in this room.
