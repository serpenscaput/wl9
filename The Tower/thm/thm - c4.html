Castle 4

Take out the "sub" mutant and the guard with your knife. Shoot the SS and get his machine gun, there is an additional one here too and an ammo box a little to the south. Try to get them before finishing off the remaining enemies as the come.

Beyond a "Dead Guard Trick" you get into the big southern room with explodable barrels. Get well into this room before opening fire at anyone or else the enemies in the south western room may be alarmed to early. And leave the barrels. Take up position in front of this unlocked door, open it, shoot the single barrel, kill the guards and go straight in. Then turn around and blow up the barrels as the many enemies from the south western room are coming. But look out for others coming from the room to the north.

Make it into the south eastern hallway and open fire. Run back to this room and shoot enemies in any of the bronze doorways to gain access to the cross.

Go past this death chalice and clean out the north eastern area. Then locate this pushwall by the health chalice. continue
