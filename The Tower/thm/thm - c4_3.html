Castle 4

Enter this area holding a cross. Shoot the SS and the mutant beyond the pushwall. Enter the elevator.

It will take you down to a secret part of the dungeons. Kill the mutants and the "sub" mutants and find the silver key in the first cell.

Go by the "Dead Guard Trick" but pick up the ammo found a little further first. Then do away with the mutants. If you missed the silver key go past the pushwalls to the right. Shooting the barrel will alarm a mutant behind the silver door.

Kill this SS and get the health chalice. You may need it.

The innermost cell is a one way door. Wade through the two pools of poison in front of it.

Take the middle one way cell door. Kill the remaining enemies. Pushwalls let you into the other cells from the other way.

Enter the elevator to the secret castle. back
