- Enemies & Bosses

These are the enemies that can be found in the mod:

 Guards - Average speed, slow firing and weak. Although, these buggers can be dangerous in big groups.

 Stabber - Replaces the dog. Above average speed, fast stabbing and medium health. Not dangerous, but irritating.

 Robot - Average speed, fast firing rate and medium health. Very dangerous in groups.

 SS - Slow but powerful elite soldiers. Some of them wear bullet-proof vests, so don't be surprised if one of them die of two shots while another die of five shots. Extremly dangerous in groups.

 Officer - Fast in both firing and speed. These guys are very dangerous in groups.

Here are the bosses that you'll encounter through the game:

 Adolf "Die Todeskanone" Grosse - Weilding two big chainguns and a thick, brown armor. This is the youngest child in the Grosse family. He's not only in Todesdrache to defend the grail but also to avenge his older brothers and his older sister, who were killed by B.J.

 Colonel Müller - This is the nazi-colonel himself! With one chaingun and rocketlauncher in his hands, he's very dangerous. Strafe and shoot!

 Cabeza De La Mirda - This fast, italian madman is only in castle Todesdrache for one reason, to kill you! This guy's a toughie, so you better have some good technique before you open the door and release this beast.

 Himmelpferd "Die Todesmaschine" 900-X - The big bulky yellow robot has returned from robot-hell to protect the grail at all costs. You'd better watch out, he's got 4 shoulder-mounted rocketlaunchers and two chainguns, and they're all aimed towards you!
