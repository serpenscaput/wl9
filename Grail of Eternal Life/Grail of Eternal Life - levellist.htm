- The Levels

Here's a list of all the levels and some information on them:

Level 1: The Great Forest
This is the only way to get into the castle, it might be dangerous but it's the only way. I recommend using the sword here so that you don't alert all the guards in the forest, though, it's kinda hard to defeat all those enemies with the sword...

Level 2: Castle Mines

This is the deep, dark mines that leads to the castles basement. Be aware of the stabbers running in here!

Level 3: Castle Basement

You're finally inside the castle, but there's still a long way to go.

Level 4: Castle Entrance

This is the castle's main entrance and it's a very dangerous floor. Watch out for the robots lurking behind one of the doors in the beggining!

Boss 1: Adolf Grosse

The levels are getting tougher and tougher. It's time to kill another Grosse family-member!

Info on the rest of the levels' coming soon.
