You start here. If you wait a little the nearest guard will have turned and the other will be out of sight when you enter the hallway. Take out both with your knife.

You may want to try to clean out these two rooms right away especially to get the health chalices in the upper room. But bear in mind: To grab ammo when you don't have the chance to use it may prove very unwise.

Finish these two guards to clear the way to the elevator to the basement. Watch out for the bomb among the ammo clips when you pick them up later.
