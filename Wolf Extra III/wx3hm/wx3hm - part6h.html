Part 6

You may now choose in what order you do the next three operations.

You can go down to the ground floor and clean it out although that is something you may do later. It is not necessary to do at all but you will probably gain some ammo from it.

If you do, start by shooting the remaining enemies in this room with your silent pistol, if you have not already done so.

Then choose the chaingun and start the fight by going through this door. Remember that both the green and the yellow ghost may be around.

It is an especially good reason to perform the last described operation at this point if the red ghost was a nuisance on the floor above. You may then make a detour and trick it into this room where it will probably stay while you reenter the central part of floor 2. continue
